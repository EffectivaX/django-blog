About Me

I am Andile. That's just about all I can say about myself, hahaha. Well I am not sure what is expected of me but ok, let me see.

First and foremost, I am your typical nerd. I try to deny it as quick as possible but it is the fact. I am a self driven individual, a goal getter. I believe in hardwork, well simply because the more you work the closer you get to your intended goal or goals. I have a lot of ambitious goals and therefore I have no other choice but to work hard and smarter too.

By training I am a technologist, by default I am a avid learner. I love computers and technology. I am happy to be part of the generation that got to live in the pic of technology. My interests mainly are in programming. I have experience and some level of exposure in some of the world renowned technologies today including Python, Nodejs, Reactjs, Typescript, PHP, Visual Basic .NET, C++ and JavaScript. The key technology areas I am most fascinated by including web app development, cloud computing, Internet of Things (IoT), Embedded Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Data Visualisation, Blockchain and Quantum Computing.

I am a self starter, a workaholic if you like. My dreams are to be a renowned figure in the technology space. I dream of running my own companies some day soon.
